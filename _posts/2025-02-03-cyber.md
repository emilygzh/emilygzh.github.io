This past weekend, 3 Duke Cyber Policy teams flew to Austin, Texas to compete in the Atlantic Council's Cyber 9/12 Strategy Challenge, hosted by UT Austin Strauss Center. 

In December, we recieved an intelligence packet containing evidence of two seemingly separate cyber incidents in the US. Over the next month or so, we dove into the case and prepared both a 2-page and 1-page document containing a brief on the situation and a series of crisis response recommendations for the US government.
